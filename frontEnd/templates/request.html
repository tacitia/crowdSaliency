  • SearchParty

Find interesting parts of your large image, quickly.

Need help searching for interesting objects or regions in your large images? Tired of investing your own person-hours looking at pictures when you could be working on other things?

SearchParty is a web service that recruits online workers on Amazon Mechanical Turk to help find things in large images. You give us your image and a hint at what's interesting in it, and we'll recruit workers and notify you when we've searched your image.

« Back to home

How it works?

When you upload an image to SearchParty, we automatically create search tasks and distribute them to searchers on Amazon Mechanical Turk. Each searcher explores through your image independently. After lots of searchers have participated, SearchParty aggregates findings from these searches into a heat map that shows you interesting regions.
